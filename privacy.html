Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ReviewSehat Studio ("ReviewSehat Studio", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service available at https://santoso.me and https://affiliate-automation.replit.app (collectively, the "Service").

ReviewSehat Studio is an AI-powered content automation tool that generates TikTok voiceover scripts, captions, and promotional videos for health and beauty affiliate marketers operating on the Shopee Indonesia platform.

By using ReviewSehat Studio, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account credentials. Access to the ReviewSehat Studio dashboard is protected by a password. We store a hashed session token to keep you signed in. We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or personal names unless you voluntarily provide them.

Product data. When you enter Shopee affiliate links or add products to your library, ReviewSehat Studio stores that product information (name, price, image URL, affiliate link, commission rate, and category) to generate content on your behalf.

Generated content. TikTok scripts, captions, video render results, and scheduling data produced by ReviewSehat Studio are stored in our system so you can access them from the dashboard. This content belongs to you.

TikTok account data. When you connect your TikTok account to ReviewSehat Studio via TikTok's OAuth 2.0 authorization flow, we store only the access token and refresh token issued by TikTok. We do not store your TikTok username, password, or personal profile data beyond what is necessary to post content on your behalf.

Usage logs. We collect standard server access logs (IP address, request timestamps, HTTP status codes) for security monitoring and error diagnosis. These logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days and are not shared with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

ReviewSehat Studio uses the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Service, including generating TikTok scripts and captions
  • Render promotional videos from your product images
  • Post content to TikTok on your behalf using the TikTok Content Posting API
  • Authenticate your access to the dashboard
  • Schedule content across your selected daily posting slots
  • Diagnose technical issues and maintain system security
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of AI-generated content

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. TikTok Integration

ReviewSehat Studio integrates with the TikTok Content Posting API to post videos directly to your TikTok account. When you authorize ReviewSehat Studio to access your TikTok account:

  • We request only the minimum permissions necessary: video.upload and video.publish
  • We store your TikTok access token and refresh token securely on our servers
  • We do not access, read, or store your TikTok messages, followers, or analytics
  • You can revoke ReviewSehat Studio's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's Settings → Security → Authorized Apps
  • Any data shared with TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy

5. Third-Party Services

Anthropic Claude. Script and caption generation is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Product details you submit are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that data.

TikTok. This application integrates with the TikTok developer platform via the Content Posting API. We do not store TikTok account passwords. Any data shared with TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

Shopee. We access publicly available product information from Shopee based on the affiliate links you provide. We do not access or store your Shopee account credentials.

ElevenLabs. Voiceover audio generation may be powered by ElevenLabs API. Script text is sent to ElevenLabs for audio synthesis. ElevenLabs' privacy policy applies to that data.

6. Data Retention

Generated content entries are retained until you delete them from the dashboard. Session tokens expire after 90 days of inactivity. Server access logs are deleted after 30 days. TikTok access tokens are deleted if you revoke authorization or close your account.

7. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Dashboard access is protected by password authentication. We use industry-standard practices to protect stored data from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of all data associated with your account at any time by contacting us at admin@santoso.me. Because ReviewSehat Studio does not collect personal identity information beyond session tokens, most user data can be removed by deleting your content entries from the dashboard directly.

9. Children's Privacy

ReviewSehat Studio is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of ReviewSehat Studio after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
